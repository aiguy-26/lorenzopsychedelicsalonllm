Psychedelic Resources

  • Psychedelic Salon – The original home of the Psychedelic Salon podcast series. In-depth talks and interviews with thinkers like Terence McKenna, Rupert Sheldrake, Graham Hancock, and more.
  • Erowid – A comprehensive online library of user reports, research, and reference materials on psychoactive substances.
  • MAPS (Multidisciplinary Association for Psychedelic Studies) – A nonprofit driving clinical research into MDMA, psilocybin, and other healing molecules.
  • Chacruna Institute – Scholarly articles, cultural context, and advocacy around plant medicines.
  • Beckley Foundation – UK-based think-tank and research hub for psychedelics and consciousness science.
  • SAMHSA – U.S. government resources on mental health, addiction, and substance-use treatment.

Community Resources

  • r/Psychedelics – Peer-to-peer discussion, trip reports, research news and harm-reduction tips.
  • Shroomery – Forums dedicated to mushroom cultivation, safe use, and community support.
  • Psymposia – Interviews, journalism, and multimedia spotlighting modern psychedelic culture.

Books, Talks & Media

  • How to Change Your Mind by Michael Pollan – The modern resurgence of clinical and cultural interest in psychedelics.
  • The Doors of Perception by Aldous Huxley – A foundational exploration of mescaline and the nature of human perception.
  • Food of the Gods by Terence McKenna – McKenna’s thesis on the role of psychoactive plants in human evolution.

Legal & Policy

  • NORML – Advocacy for sensible cannabis policy and broader drug-law reform.
  • ACLU – Psychedelic Reform – Civil-liberties perspective on decriminalization and equitable reform.
  • Decriminalize Nature – Coalition working to decriminalize entheogenic plants and fungi at the municipal level.
