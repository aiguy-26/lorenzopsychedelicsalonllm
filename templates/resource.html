Psychedelic Resources

  • Erowid - A comprehensive and trusted online resource dedicated to the documentation and exploration of psychoactive substances. Erowid offers user reports, research articles, and detailed descriptions of effects, dosages, and safety considerations.
  • MAPS (Multidisciplinary Association for Psychedelic Studies) - A leading nonprofit organization that conducts research into the medical and therapeutic benefits of psychedelics. MAPS plays a pivotal role in advocacy, policy reform, and clinical trials related to the use of substances like MDMA and psilocybin in treating mental health conditions.
  • SAMHSA (Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration) - A government body offering resources and programs for individuals struggling with mental health and substance abuse issues. SAMHSA provides national helplines and guides for treatment options, making it an essential resource for those seeking support.

Community Resources

  • Psychedelics Reddit - A thriving online community where individuals share personal experiences, research, and discussions related to psychedelics. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced user, this subreddit offers a wealth of knowledge and support.
  • Shroomery - A popular online forum that focuses on psychedelic experiences, particularly the cultivation and safe use of psilocybin mushrooms. It also offers resources for growing, consuming, and learning about psychedelics.

Books, Talks & Media

  • How to Change Your Mind by Michael Pollan - A comprehensive exploration of the history, science, and therapeutic potential of psychedelics. Pollan delves into the resurgence of psychedelic research and shares his personal experiences, offering insights into how these substances can alter consciousness and treat various mental health conditions.
  • The Doors of Perception by Aldous Huxley - A classic essay detailing Huxley's mescaline-induced journey, providing profound insights into human perception and consciousness. This work has been influential in both literary and philosophical circles, offering a glimpse into the potential of psychedelics to expand awareness.
  • The Electric Kool-Aid Acid Test by Tom Wolfe - An engaging narrative following Ken Kesey and the Merry Pranksters on their cross-country bus trip, capturing the essence of the 1960s counterculture and the psychedelic movement. Wolfe's journalistic style brings the era to life, highlighting the social and cultural impact of psychedelics.

  • Food of the Gods by Terence McKenna - An exploration of the historical and cultural significance of psychoactive plants. McKenna examines the role of these substances in human evolution, religion, and society, offering a thought-provoking perspective on their impact.
  • The Cosmic Serpent by Jeremy Narby - Narby's work delves into the connections between DNA and ancient shamanic knowledge, proposing that indigenous shamans have long understood the molecular basis of life through their psychedelic experiences.
  • Be Here Now by Ram Dass - A spiritual classic that chronicles Ram Dass's journey from Harvard professor to spiritual teacher, offering insights into meditation, mindfulness, and the transformative power of psychedelics.
  • The Psychedelic Experience by Timothy Leary, Ralph Metzner, and Richard Alpert - A guide to navigating the psychedelic experience, drawing parallels with the Tibetan Book of the Dead. This work provides practical advice for those seeking to explore altered states of consciousness.
  • The Way of the Psychonaut by Stanislav Grof - A comprehensive exploration of the human unconscious and the therapeutic potential of non-ordinary states of consciousness. Grof's work is foundational in transpersonal psychology and psychedelic therapy.

Legal Information

  • NORML - The National Organization for the Reform of Marijuana Laws advocates for the legal and responsible use of cannabis. This site provides information on cannabis policy, updates on legalization efforts, and resources for those navigating cannabis laws.
  • ACLU - Psychedelic Reform - The American Civil Liberties Union provides valuable information on the intersection of criminal justice and psychedelic laws. The ACLU works to reform policies that criminalize the use of psychedelics and advocates for a more informed, compassionate approach to drug laws.
